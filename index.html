About Me

I’m a Data Scientist with a Master’s in Data Science and a Bachelor’s in Applied Statistics, passionate about transforming complex data into meaningful insights that drive strategic decisions. With hands-on experience in Python, R, SQL, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, I specialize in data cleaning, exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, and dashboard development. Over the years, I’ve contributed to impactful projects in education, public policy, and financial reporting—analyzing datasets with 20,000+ observations, designing models to monitor inflation trends, and leveraging data to support national budget planning. I’ve also led data-driven initiatives in election technology, ensuring accuracy and transparency in reporting.
